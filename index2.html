About SANVIA

SANVIA consolidates a diversified IP estate spanning healthcare, biotechnology, food safety, and environmental protection. Our cornerstone is EQ12™—a next-generation quaternary ammonium platform— and proprietary supercritical CO₂ infusion methods that transform materials into self-protecting systems.

We license, develop, and protect intellectual property. Subsidiaries and strategic partners leverage SANVIA’s assets to accelerate product pipelines without compromising safety or performance.

Patent Estate

Five interlocking families protect synthesis, materials processing, medical and environmental applications, and cross-sector deployment. The summary below provides a non-confidential overview.

0001 — Supercritical Infusion & Composites

Issued U.S. Patent for SCF-CO₂ infusion of antimicrobials into polymers, decellularized tissues, and composites.

Use cases: medical implants, advanced materials.

0002 — EQ12™ Synthesis & Composition

Novel manufacturing route to C12-dominant quaternary ammonium compound (EQ12™) with high purity.

Use cases: pharma-grade actives, surface hygiene, materials integration.

0003 — Medical & Wound Applications

Topical and irrigant applications for wound care and surgical fields; favorable safety profile at use levels.

Use cases: surgical irrigants, wound cleansers, device care.

0004 — Materials & Textiles (SCF Integration)

Self-disinfecting respirators, filters, and textiles via supercritical impregnation without impairing performance.

Use cases: PPE, HVAC, cleanrooms, transportation.

0005 — Cross-Sector Biological Applications

Food safety (live & processed), agriculture, and feed protection at ultra-low concentrations.

Use cases: shellfish, sprouts, produce, meat, animal feed.
