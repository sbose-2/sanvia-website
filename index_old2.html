About SANVIA

SANVIA holds intellectual property assets developed through years of research in antimicrobial science and advanced material processes. Founded by Dr. Chad Roy and Dr. Joe Buell, the company's portfolio includes patents related to quaternary ammonium compounds and supercritical fluid technologies.

As an IP holding company, SANVIA licenses these technologies to qualified partners and subsidiaries for development and commercialization across various industries and applications.
